Experience

I started my journey as a Photographer back in 2018 when I got my first camera. I started clicking photos of random objects without any story or motivde behind the photos. Having no prior knowledge about the field or the art form, I learned everything practically by hit and trial. With constant experimenting, I found my genre - Wildlife Photography. Cut to 2023, after 5 years of self learning, I am a featured photographer in various platforms like BBC Earth, Nikon Asia, Nature in Focus and MP Tourism.
